About

Hi, I'm Monica, a passionate web developer, fashion designer, and special needs educator. I have a deep love for combining creativity with technology, and I take pride in every project I work on. With my experience in multiple fields, I aim to bring unique perspectives to every challenge I face, whether it's designing beautiful clothing, building user-friendly websites, or nurturing special-needs students.

Services

  1. Web Development

    Providing custom websites and applications to suit your business or personal needs. From designing layouts to coding interactive features, I specialize in building fast, responsive, and user-friendly websites.

  2. Fashion Design

    Offering tailored fashion designs, focusing on unique, creative, and comfortable clothing. Whether you're looking for everyday wear or something for a special occasion, my designs are made to fit your style and comfort.

  3. Special Needs Education

    Providing specialized education for children with unique learning needs. With experience in developing personalized learning plans and engaging activities, I strive to make learning accessible and enjoyable for every child.

  4. Consultation Services

    Offering one-on-one consultations to help you with your web development or fashion design needs. Whether you need advice on website structure, improving user experience, or creating a unique fashion line, I am here to guide you through the process.

  5. Workshop/Training

    Providing hands-on workshops and training sessions for individuals or small groups interested in web development, fashion design, or special needs education. These workshops are tailored to both beginners and advanced learners, with practical, real-world applications.

  6. Custom Orders

    Offering custom clothing designs tailored to your unique style and preferences. Whether it's for an event, work, or casual wear, I provide personalized fashion solutions designed to meet your exact needs.
